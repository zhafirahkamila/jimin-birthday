Chapter III

To Jimin

He is more than an idol.
He became a comfort during our hardest days,
a smile we searched for,
a voice we returned to,
and a home we never expected to find.

May you always find reasons to smile,
just as you have given us countless reasons to smile.

Don’t be too hard on yourself.

You have done enough.
You are enough.

May this new chapter bring you peace,
comfort, happiness,
and all the love you deserve.

Thank you for growing with us.
Thank you for staying.
Thank you, Park Jimin.
